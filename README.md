 

# Airbnb Global Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## Executive Summary

Airbnb is one of the world’s leading platforms for short-term accommodation and travel experiences. As the platform expanded globally, a large volume of data has been generated around listings, hosts, property types, cities, and customer reviews. However, raw operational data alone cannot provide meaningful insights without proper analysis and visualization.

This Business Intelligence project analyzes Airbnb listing data using **Power BI** to uncover insights related to global listing performance, host activity, city-level market share, and property type distribution.

The dashboard transforms raw Airbnb data into an **interactive visual report** that helps stakeholders understand:

* Global listing growth trends
* Market share distribution across cities
* Host participation and activity
* Property type performance
* Review distribution and engagement levels

The goal is to deliver **clear, data-driven insights** that help understand how Airbnb listings evolve across cities and time.

# 🔍 5. Key Insights

### Insight 1 — Listing Growth Over Time

Airbnb experienced rapid growth after its launch in **2008**, reaching a peak expansion phase around **2015** when the highest number of new listings were added.

---

### Insight 2 — Regulatory Impact on Growth

In **2016–2017**, growth slowed due to **tightening local regulations in several cities**. Despite this, Airbnb managed to achieve profitability during this period.

---

### Insight 3 — City-Level Market Concentration

Cities such as **Paris, New York City, and Sydney** account for **nearly half of the total listings** and **59% of total reviews**, showing strong market concentration.

---

### Insight 4 — Paris Leading the Market

Paris holds the **largest number of listings and reviews**, possibly due to the high cost of hotels compared to Airbnb accommodations.

---

### Insight 5 — COVID-19 Impact

Growth slowed significantly around **2019–2020**, largely due to the **COVID-19 pandemic**, which affected global travel and tourism.

# 📌 6. Recommendations

Based on the insights from the dashboard, the following recommendations can be made:

### Platform Strategy

* Expand listings in **underrepresented cities** to balance market concentration.

### Host Engagement

* Encourage new hosts in emerging markets through **incentives and onboarding programs**.

### Market Expansion

* Focus on cities with **high tourism demand but low listing supply**.

### Property Optimization

* Promote **high-demand property types** such as entire homes and private rooms.
